## Eligibility Confirmation

By signing this addendum, the Partner confirms that it meets all eligibility requirements for OSSI participation:

- [ ] Active Gold or Silver annual sponsorship of the Commonhaus Foundation;
- [ ] Demonstrated history of delivering security and compatibility fixes for EOL open source software;
- [ ] Current SOC 2 Type 1 and Type 2 certification(s);
- [ ] Demonstrated substantive prior work on at least two (2) CVEs affecting EOL open source software relevant to Commonhaus projects, including remediation, coordinated disclosure, or delivery of fixes

## Partner Commitments

The Partner agrees that, for the EOL versions of open source software it elects to support under OSSI, it will use commercially reasonable efforts to:

- identify, triage, and remediate security vulnerabilities;
- deliver compatibility fixes or guidance for users who cannot yet upgrade; and
- coordinate vulnerability disclosure with affected Commonhaus projects and upstream maintainers before public disclosure where feasible and appropriate.

## Foundation Commitments

The Foundation agrees to:

- list the Partner on the [OSSI page](https://www.commonhaus.org/about/ossi/) for the duration of this addendum;
- require participating projects that identify EOL versions to link to the OSSI page as a resource for users seeking continued support; and
- permit projects, at their discretion, to reference individual Partner support offerings directly, provided any such references are fair, accurate, and non-misleading.

## Term and Renewal

This addendum is effective for the term of the underlying Corporate Sponsorship Agreement and renews automatically with it, provided eligibility requirements continue to be met.

Either party may terminate OSSI participation with 30 days written notice without affecting the underlying sponsorship agreement.

## Governance

Participation in OSSI does not grant governance rights within the Foundation or its projects, authority over project roadmaps or technical direction, or endorsement beyond inclusion in this initiative.

All other terms of the Corporate Sponsorship Agreement remain in effect.
